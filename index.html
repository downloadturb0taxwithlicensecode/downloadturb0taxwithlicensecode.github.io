Download TurboTax with License Code



If you’ve purchased TurboTax and received a license code, you’re just a few simple steps away from downloading and installing the software. This guide will walk you through the entire process, ensuring a smooth setup so you can focus on preparing your taxes.

What is a License Code?

Your license code is a unique 16-character combination of letters and numbers that confirms your purchase of TurboTax. It’s required to verify your ownership and activate the software. You can find your license code in one of these places:

  • Email Confirmation: If you purchased online, check the confirmation email sent to your registered email address.
  • Retail Package: If you purchased a physical copy, the code will be on the installation card or inside the package.

Step-by-Step Guide to Download TurboTax

  1. Visit Download TurboTax with License Code Website Open your web browser and go to TurboTax Official Website. This is the safest and most reliable place to download the software.
  2. Sign In or Create an Intuit Account
    • If you already have an account with Intuit, log in using your existing credentials.
    • If you’re new, click on “Sign Up” or “Create Account,” and provide the necessary details such as your email address, name, and a secure password.
  3. Enter Your License Code Once logged in, look for the option to download TurboTax. When prompted, enter your 16-character license code. This step verifies your purchase and ensures you’re downloading the correct version.
  4. Select the Right Version of TurboTax Depending on your purchase, choose the version of TurboTax that suits your needs (e.g., Basic, Deluxe, Premier, or Self-Employed). Ensure it matches the license code you’ve entered.
  5. Start the Download Process After verification, click the download button. The software will begin downloading to your computer. Make sure your internet connection is stable to avoid interruptions.
  6. Locate the Downloaded File Once the download is complete, locate the file in your device’s “Downloads” folder or wherever your browser saves downloaded files.
  7. Install TurboTax Double-click the downloaded file to start the installation process. Follow the on-screen instructions, including agreeing to the terms and conditions, selecting the installation location, and setting up shortcuts.
  8. Activate TurboTax After installation, open TurboTax and sign in with your Intuit account. If prompted, enter the license code again to activate the software.
  9. Get Ready to File Your Taxes You’re all set! Once the software is activated, you can start entering your financial details, calculating returns, and preparing your tax filings.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Invalid License Code: Double-check the code you’ve entered. Ensure there are no typos, and verify if the code matches the product you’ve purchased.
  • Download Issues: If the download doesn’t start or gets interrupted, check your internet connection or try using a different browser.
